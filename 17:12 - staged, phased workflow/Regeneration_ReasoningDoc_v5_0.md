## 1. Alignment Pattern

**Pattern**: Clinical Empathy

**Core Dynamic**: Third-person limited access through medical perspective combines with objective yet compassionate stance to create therapeutic distance that enables deeper understanding of trauma.

**Reader Effect**: Readers experience psychological wounds through both clinical observation and intimate consciousness, creating intellectual comprehension paired with emotional resonance.

## 2. Macro Variable Justifications

**Narrative Voice**: The TPL (Third Person Limited) through Rivers' medical perspective creates professional distance while allowing intimate access to consciousness. INT (Internal Focalization) enables readers to experience characters' psychological states from within. REL (Reliable) narration establishes trust essential for clinical observation. IMM (Immediate) temporal distance maintains urgency of treatment sessions. SOME intrusion allows for necessary contextual medical insights without breaking therapeutic intimacy.

**Narrative Structure**: LIN (Linear) chronology mirrors the progressive nature of psychological treatment. MONO (Monoplot) architecture maintains focus on the central therapeutic relationships at Craiglockhart. VAR (Variable) pacing reflects the irregular rhythms of psychological healing. MEDIAS beginning places readers directly into ongoing treatment, establishing immediate clinical context.

**Rhetorical Voice**: OBJ (Objective) stance provides the clinical detachment necessary for medical observation while maintaining compassion. FORM (Formal) register reflects professional medical discourse. SIT (Situational) irony emerges from the contrast between military expectations and psychological reality. STRONG pathos ensures emotional connection despite clinical distance.

**Rhetorical Structure**: TRANS (Transformational) alignment tracks psychological healing journeys. TIGHT N-R relationship ensures clinical observations directly serve character development. CHAR (Character) mechanism grounds transformation in individual psychological profiles. ACCUMULATION mediation builds understanding through layered therapeutic insights.

## 3. Device Selections by Freytag Section

**Exposition**: Third-Person Limited, Metaphor, Juxtaposition, Imagery, Symbolism, Dialogue, Foreshadowing — establish clinical perspective while introducing psychological landscapes. Juxtaposition contrasts war trauma with hospital safety, Metaphor translates psychological states into comprehensible terms, Symbolism embeds deeper meanings in clinical observations.

**Rising Action**: Juxtaposition, Symbolism, Imagery, Foreshadowing, Third-Person Limited, Metaphor, Dialogue — develop therapeutic relationships through contrasts between patients' internal worlds and external presentations. Foreshadowing builds tension around treatment outcomes, while Imagery creates visceral connection to psychological states.

**Climax**: Third-Person Limited, Symbolism, Juxtaposition, Flashback, Imagery, Metaphor, Foreshadowing, Motif — intensify psychological breakthroughs through layered consciousness access. Flashback provides direct trauma experience, Motif reinforces recurring psychological patterns, while clinical perspective maintains therapeutic framework.

**Falling Action**: Third-Person Limited, Metaphor, Juxtaposition, Symbolism, Imagery, Internal Monologue, Foreshadowing, Motif — pivot toward resolution through deepened internal access. Internal Monologue allows more intimate psychological exploration, while maintaining clinical observation framework through Third-Person Limited.

**Resolution**: Metaphor, Symbolism, Imagery, Juxtaposition, Dramatic Irony, Internal Monologue — complete the clinical empathy pattern by balancing professional assessment with human understanding. Dramatic Irony highlights the gap between clinical success and ongoing human struggle.

## 4. Alignment Effect

This Clinical Empathy alignment creates a unique reader experience where psychological trauma becomes both intellectually comprehensible and emotionally resonant through the fusion of medical objectivity with intimate access to consciousness. The TPL perspective through Rivers' clinical lens, combined with OBJ stance and FORM register, establishes professional credibility that legitimizes the deep psychological exploration. Meanwhile, devices like Internal Monologue and Imagery create visceral connection to characters' inner worlds, while Juxtaposition and Symbolism reveal the complex relationships between trauma, treatment, and healing. The TRANS alignment ensures that clinical observations serve character transformation rather than mere documentation, while ACCUMULATION mediation builds empathy through layered understanding. This combination allows readers to experience psychological wounds with both the analytical distance necessary for comprehension and the emotional proximity required for genuine compassion, creating therapeutic reading that mirrors the novel's own healing methodology.
