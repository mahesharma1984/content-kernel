## 1. Alignment Pattern

**Pattern**: Claustrophobic Intensification

**Core Dynamic**: First-person limited perspective creates mounting psychological pressure through Silvie's increasingly trapped awareness of familial and social dynamics she cannot escape.

**Reader Effect**: Readers experience escalating tension and suffocation as they become locked within Silvie's constricted viewpoint of deteriorating circumstances.

## 2. Macro Variable Justifications

**Narrative Voice**: The FP (first-person) POV confines readers entirely within Silvie's consciousness, while INT (internal) focalization ensures no external perspective can relieve this confinement. REL (reliable) narration means we trust Silvie's perceptions, making her entrapment our entrapment. IMM (immediate) temporal distance eliminates reflective distance, trapping us in the present moment of her psychological pressure. NONE intrusion prevents any authorial relief valve.

**Narrative Structure**: LIN (linear) chronology creates inexorable forward momentum toward crisis, while MONO (single plot) architecture provides no subplot escape routes. VAR (variable) pacing alternates between slow psychological build-up and sudden moments of intensity. The MEDIAS (in medias res) beginning thrusts readers immediately into an already constrained situation.

**Rhetorical Voice**: SUBJ (subjective) stance roots everything in Silvie's limited perspective. The tense, claustrophobic tone directly manifests the pattern's core dynamic. COLL (colloquial) register makes Silvie's entrapment feel immediate and authentic. SIT (situational) irony emerges from the gap between the archaeological experiment's scholarly pretensions and its psychological violence.

**Rhetorical Structure**: TRANS (transformative) alignment signals that the reading experience will fundamentally alter our understanding. TIGHT N-R relationship ensures maximum transfer of Silvie's psychological state to readers. CHAR (character-driven) mechanism focuses pressure through Silvie's consciousness. ACCUMULATION mediation builds psychological weight incrementally until it becomes unbearable.

## 4. Alignment Effect

This alignment transforms readers into psychological prisoners alongside Silvie, creating an experience of mounting claustrophobia that mirrors her entrapment within toxic family dynamics. The FP/INT narrative voice codes lock us within her perspective while REL reliability ensures we trust her increasingly desperate perceptions, and the TIGHT N-R relationship transfers her psychological pressure directly to us. The MONO architecture eliminates escape routes while LIN chronology creates inexorable momentum toward crisis, supported by SIT irony that exposes the gap between academic pretensions and psychological violence. Devices execute this pattern through escalating cycles: Imagery and Symbolism intensify environmental and historical pressure, Juxtaposition sharpens unbearable contrasts, while Repetition and Internal Monologue create obsessive psychological loops that mirror Silvie's entrapment.
